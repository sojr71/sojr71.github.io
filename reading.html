Books I'm Currently Reading

The God of the Woods has given me a real book hangover, so I haven't picked up a new book just yet. I am, however, listening to Catherine Price's How to Break up With Your Phone (this html-learning endeavor is a direct result of this choice) which is perhaps the most written-in-2018-by-a-millenial book ever written. It's not bad! It provides some solid reasoning as to why you should consider "breaking up with your phone" and a pretty useful 30 day road map about how you might accomplish that feat. I'm looking forward to continuing to follow it.
